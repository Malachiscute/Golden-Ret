Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are such delightful dogs! They're known for their friendly and gentle temperament, making them excellent family pets and therapy dogs. Their beautiful golden coats and warm eyes are just part of their charm. They're also intelligent and eager to please, which makes them relatively easy to train. Plus, their love for play and exercise means they're always up for a game of fetch or a long walk. Overall, they're a beloved breed for good reason!
